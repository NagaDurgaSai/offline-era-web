HOW IT WORKS

Zero config.
Just connect.

UDP multicast handles discovery. TCP handles everything else. No router config, no port forwarding, no accounts.

01

Connect to the same network

WiFi, ethernet, or hotspot. As long as devices share a subnet, Offline Era finds them automatically via UDP multicast.

02

Devices discover each other

No pairing, no accounts. A stable UUID identifies each device across DHCP reassignments and reinstalls.

03

Start messaging

Open a chat, drag files, check who's online. Everything is direct TCP — nothing leaves your local network.
